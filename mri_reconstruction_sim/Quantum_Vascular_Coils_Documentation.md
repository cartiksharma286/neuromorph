## Mathematical Framework Summary

### Feynman Path Integrals
Path amplitude: $A[\text{path}] = \int \exp(iS[\text{path}]/\hbar) \mathcal{D}[\text{path}]$

### Ramanujan Modular Forms
Theta function: $\theta(q) = 1 + 2\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} q^{n^2}$

### Elliptic Integrals
- Complete first kind: $K(m) = \int_0^{\pi/2} \frac{d\theta}{\sqrt{1-m\sin^2\theta}}$
- Complete second kind: $E(m) = \int_0^{\pi/2} \sqrt{1-m\sin^2\theta} \, d\theta$

### Special Functions
- Bessel: $J_\nu(x)$, Struve: $H_\nu(x)$, Kelvin: ber, bei
- Legendre: $P_l^m(x)$, Hermite: $H_n(x)$, Laguerre: $L_n^\alpha(x)$
- Hypergeometric: ${}_2F_1(a,b;c;z)$, ${}_1F_1(a;b;z)$
